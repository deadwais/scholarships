MPOWER's Global Citizen Scholarship

By Scholarships Canada Modified on June 15, 2022
Tags : Scholarships | Travel

Thrive as an international student with a grand prize scholarship of US $10,000.

5903Save


If you're an international student, you've probably discovered first hand just how hard finding a scholarship can be. International tuition is often twice as high as domestic fees, meaning you have major bills to pay — and comparably fewer scholarship options to help you.

That's where MPOWER Financing steps in, with its annual Global Citizen Scholarships. Built specifically for international students, this award is open to students studying abroad in the US or Canada — and though MPOWER offers loan support to international students, you don't need to be an MPOWER borrower to apply.

MPOWER Global Citizen Scholarship details

The Global Citizen Scholarship has three prize levels:

  • Grand Prize: $10,000
  • Runner-Up: $2,000
  • Honourable Mention: $2,000

That means you have three chances to win some cash for school. But don't wait to apply: the deadline is July 15, 2022.

Am I eligible for the MPOWER Global Citizen Scholarship?

If you're an international student who's studying full time at an accepted Canadian or U.S. school, you could receive a scholarship worth up to US $10,000. Here are the specifics:

  • Be accepted or enrolled full-time at one of the 400+ Canadian or U.S. schools supported by MPOWER
  • Be an international student legally permitted to study in Canada or the United States

That's it! You don't need to be an MPOWER borrower to apply.

Remember, this scholarship is specifically for international and DACA students, meaning Canadians studying in Canada, and U.S. citizens studying in the U.S. are not eligible for this award.

How do I apply for the MPOWER Global Citizen Scholarship?

The application process is simple — no stress required!

Step 1: Review the application

Browse the MPOWER Global Citizen Scholarship webpage to be sure you have all the details.

Step 2: Respond to the essay question

In 500 words or less, write an essay, poem, slideshow, or other written work about:

  • The impacts you've made on your home community, or the broader international scientific community
  • How you'll continue to make an impact after you graduate
  • How your education will help you achieve your goals

Easy!

Step 3: Proofread and submit

Ensure your application is 100% complete, accurate, and truthful, and then have a friend or teacher look it over before you submit. Correct any spelling or punctuation errors, and send your application in before the July 15 deadline!

Now's your chance to receive a scholarship worth up to US $10,000 for your studies as an international student. You could be the next MPOWER Global Citizen Scholarship recipient, so don't wait — apply for a scholarship now. The deadline is July 15, 2022!
